This repository is a research and educational resource. Be kind, be constructive, and assume good intent.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and welcoming.
- Provide constructive, specific feedback.
- Do not harass, demean, or insult others.

## Scope

These expectations apply in issues, pull requests, discussions, and any community spaces tied to this
repository.

## Reporting

If someone is being disruptive or harmful, contact the maintainer (see [`SUPPORT.md`](SUPPORT.md)).

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove content or restrict participation to keep the space safe and productive.
